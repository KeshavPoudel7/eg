Keshav Poudel is an aerospace engineer with a lifelong dream of becoming a pilot. Born in a small village in Bardiya, Nepal, Keshav's humble beginnings shaped his determined character and work ethic. Despite limited resources, his passion for aviation and space exploration drove him to excel academically, particularly in mathematics and sciences.

Known for his kind eyes and humble demeanor, Keshav is not only dedicated to his professional pursuits but also to helping others. He regularly volunteers his time and knowledge to inspire young students interested in STEM fields. An avid sports enthusiast, he enjoys football, cricket, swimming, and exploring nature through trekking and hiking. His deep love for his country is matched only by his fascination with space and technological innovation.
